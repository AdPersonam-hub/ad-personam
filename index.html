About

Who we are, what we make, and the values behind the work.

Our approach

We produce work that brings professional artistic standards into public life — from intimate theatre productions to large-scale seasonal events. Our focus is on clarity, craft, and collaboration: strong writing, strong performers, and projects that feel meaningful to the communities they’re made with.

  • Artist-led, story-first production.
  • Accessible experiences without lowering ambition.
  • Partnerships with venues, festivals, schools and local organisations.

Leadership

The company is co-founded by theatre director, producer, actor and educator Peter McCamley. Peter’s practice spans directing, producing, performance training and community-based arts programming.
