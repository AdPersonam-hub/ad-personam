About

Who we are, what we make, and the values behind the work.

Our approach

Ad Personam Cultural Events Ltd creates theatre, music and cultural events that combine artistic excellence with genuine community connection. We work with outstanding artists and partners to produce ambitious, accessible experiences — from intimate productions and concerts to large-scale festivals and public events. Rooted in Wexford and reaching beyond, our work is driven by a belief that great art should inspire, connect and leave a lasting impression.

  • Artist-led, story-first production.
  • Accessible experiences without lowering ambition.
  • Partnerships with venues, festivals, schools and local organisations.

Leadership

The company is co-founded by theatre director, producer, actor and educator Peter McCamley and producer, actor, singing teacher and musician Aileen Donohoe. Their practice spans directing, producing, performance training and community-based arts programming.

Meet the Founders

Ad Personam Cultural Events Ltd was founded by two experienced artists whose combined careers span directing, producing, acting, music, education and cultural event production.

Peter McCamley

Co-Founder • Director • Producer • Actor • Educator

Peter is a theatre director, producer, actor and educator with more than twenty years of professional experience across theatre, opera, film and performance training. He has directed professional productions throughout Ireland and the UK, teaches acting and voice, and is committed to creating artist-led work rooted in community and place.

Visit Peter's Website

Aileen Donohoe

Co-Founder • Producer • Performer • Musician • Vocal Coach

Aileen is a performer, producer, singing teacher and musician with extensive experience creating theatre, music and community arts projects throughout Ireland. She specialises in vocal performance, music education and artistic producing, bringing warmth, creativity and organisational expertise to every Ad Personam production.
